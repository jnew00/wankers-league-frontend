🥇 Performance of the Week

Jeff Morris erupted like a cheap bottle of gas station champagne, blasting out a fat +9 and leaving the rest of you hacks wondering if you'd accidentally wandered into a junior clinic. He racked up points so fast his scorecard looked like a damn bingo sheet. That 34-point blitz wasn't just hot—it was the best league score of the entire year, a silky 74 that had everyone choking on their White Claws in stunned silence. Pretty sure he wasn't exactly following rule 4.d, either. And let's be honest, when he rolls onto Bardmoor's 7,000+ yard Black tees next week with that 30 quota, he'll probably just take all our money again.

🫠 Worst Performance of the Week

Johnny Vilela proved that golf giveth and golf taketh away. Two weeks ago, he was riding high with a +10, but Sherman Hills, easily the most forgiving track we play, wasn't feeling generous this time. A -7 isn't the end of the world, but when everyone else is padding their stats, it's just enough to remind him that even the winners have to eat a turd sandwich now and then.
