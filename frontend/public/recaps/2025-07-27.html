🥇 Performance of the Week

Bryan and Romeo brought the noise this week with matching +5 performances. Bryan played like the greens owed him money—locked in and collecting, even bagging a skin. Meanwhile Romeo just kept casually keeping pace, smiling like it was a casual stroll through Sunday brunch. It wasn’t flashy, but it was ruthless. Like a quiet hitman with a Scotty Cameron.

🫠 Worst Performance of the Week

Gary fell so hard off the scoring cliff he passed a few mountain goats on the way down. A -10 is one thing, but the real feat was keeping a smile on while watching everyone else cash skins like it was a stimulus check. Rumor is he finished the round with more swing thoughts than strokes.

📈 Other Notables

  • DJ was paired with the geri crew, featuring Dovid and Lizard. Only one of them finished the round with both shoes on, and it wasn’t either of the elder statesmen. Lizard kicked his off to play a ball out of a swamp, and Dovid decided the back nine was a great time to cosplay a pirate—one shoe on, one foot out, full audition for Long John Silver. Dovid skipped half the course and still tied Lizard at nine, which either means Dovid ’s a golfing genius or Lizard owes us all an apology.
  • DJ’s 27 from the back tees looked good on paper but felt empty inside. Without birdies, nobody drank a drop. Golf is cruel, especially sober golf.
  • Heard Q nearly became a referee between Nick and Abel. "These ahh the rues!" and "Who made you the enforcer?" rang out from the tee box like a Florida Judge Judy episode. Let me guess: Abel was massaging the lie in the rough again?
  • Jamey exploded out the gate like a chihuahua spooked by fireworks, but sputtered out faster than a generator with water in the gas tank. Four birdie looks, one conversion, and a -5 finish that looked more like a protest than a round.
  • Conner returned to the course after welcoming his daughter Charlee. He’s been keeping a newborn alive, but we’re worried the same can’t be said for his golf game. One’s thriving, the other’s decomposing.
  • Jason is off in the mountains shooting lights out—reportedly dropped a 73 with five birdies from the tips on a Robert Trent Jones course. Don’t worry though, he’ll be back to shanking chips and blading wedges with the rest of you chumps soon.

🌴 Course Conditions

It was HOT. Like, "Abel saw the devil on 16" hot. Greens rolled fair and true, but the sun was unforgiving and the shade nonexistent. It was less of a round of golf and more of a desert survival course.
