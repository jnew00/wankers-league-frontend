Data Deletion Request

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • Your social media login associations (Google, Facebook, Apple)
  • Your email address and profile information
  • Your golf scores and statistics
  • Any personal data linked to your account

Note: Historical leaderboard data may be anonymized rather than deleted to preserve league integrity.

Request Data Deletion

Alternative Contact Methods

You can also request data deletion by:

  • Email: privacy@wankersleague.com
  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Include your email address and login method used

Processing Time

Data deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.
